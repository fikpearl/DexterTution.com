WHAT WE OFFER


We strive to ensure that every student can reach their full academic potential while in the basic education system, as this is where their potential career paths are formed. Students who work with us will notice a significant improvement as a result of our dedicated assistance in their grade.

ONLINE CLASSES

We take pride in being able to provide tutoring services in the comfort of your own home. Our online classes are delivered through platforms like Zoom, Google Meet, and MSTeams.

GROUP SATURDAY CLASSES

We have collaborated with local schools to hold Saturday classes on their campuses. These classes are designed to help matriculants prepare for their final exams.

PRIVATE CLASSES

We have hired qualified and dedicated tutors all over the country. Booking a tutor is as simple as hailing an Uber. Our tutors are always available to match with potential students from all over South Africa.

AU PAIR

This service is useful for parents who have a lot on their plates and require a second right hand. Au Pairs help with daily tasks such as packing lunches for students, driving them to school, transporting them to extracurricular activities, tutoring, and more.
