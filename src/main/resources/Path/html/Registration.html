OUR MAIN OBJECTIVE ONCE YOU'VE REGISTERED


After you enroll your child in Dexter Tuition, we set three goals. The first goal is for your child to gain confidence in their learning abilities. The second goal is to improve by 20% in grades.


ACADEMIC APETITE


Once your child has regained their confidence , It is then followed by academic endurances. At this point in the journey, your child will enjoy learning and will be willing to engage in challenging concepts without hesitation.


INDEPENDENT LEARNING


We believe that the learning skills taught by our tutors eventually lead to independent learning at Dexter Tuition. We gradually guide our students through concepts until they can work them out on their own and correct their own errors.




WHAT SETS US APART




• We hire qualified tutors

Our tutors are either teaching graduates or high-achieving students with in-depth knowledge of specific subjects.



• Innovative Teaching Methods

We have spent time assessing students' common weaknesses and implementing corresponding, innovative teaching methods. We have gathered critical data that allows us to identify students' learning barriers.
